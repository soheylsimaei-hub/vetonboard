About

Created by Dr. Soheyl Simaei.

Veterinarian. Entrepreneur. Educator. Storyteller. After more than two decades in clinical practice and veterinary business across Dubai and Europe, Dr. Simaei created Vet On Board to document the world behind the profession — the science, the stories, the cities, the people, and the lessons that stay with us.

"I did not create Vet On Board to become another content creator. I created it because the profession had given me more than clinical experience. It had given me a way of seeing the world."
