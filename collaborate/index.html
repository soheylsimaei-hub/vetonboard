Collaborate with Vet On Board.

Thoughtful storytelling for brands, events, and organizations connected to animals, science, travel, and veterinary life.

Vet On Board partners selectively with brands, conferences, and organizations that value intelligent storytelling, scientific responsibility, and authentic connection with veterinary and pet-loving audiences.

If your work touches the world Vet On Board covers — animal health, veterinary science, travel, education, longevity, leadership, or culture — we are open to thoughtful collaborations that respect both your audience and ours.

Six Ways to Work Together

Collaboration formats, considered by editorial fit.

01

Brand Storytelling

Long-form features for brands whose work genuinely belongs in a veterinary editorial conversation. Always labeled. Never disguised as journalism.

02

Conference Coverage

Cinematic and editorial coverage of veterinary conferences and scientific events — the people, the rooms, the conversations between sessions.

03

Educational Storytelling

Films and essays for educational institutions, CPD providers, and academic bodies that need narrative-grade work, not corporate video.

04

Travel & Lifestyle

Editorial collaborations with destinations, hotels, and travel organizations on the texture of travel — the cities, the meals, the way places live with animals.

05

Interviews & Media

Long-form conversations with veterinary leaders, researchers, founders, and clinicians. Edited for substance, not soundbites.

06

Sponsored Field Notes

Short, observational pieces underwritten by partners whose work aligns with the topic. Always disclosed; always editorially honest.

Vet On Board only collaborates with brands and organizations that align with responsible veterinary communication, animal welfare, scientific integrity, and audience trust. Sponsored content does not replace independent clinical judgment or evidence-based veterinary advice.
